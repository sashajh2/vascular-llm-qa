6.3: Superﬁcial Venous Reﬂux and Healed Venous Leg Ulcer
In a patient with a healed venous leg ulcer (C5) and incompetent superﬁcial veins that have axial reﬂux directed
to the bed of the ulcer, we recommend ablation of the incompetent veins in addition to standard compressive therapy to prevent recurrence. [GRADE - 1; LEVEL OF EVIDENCE - C]
