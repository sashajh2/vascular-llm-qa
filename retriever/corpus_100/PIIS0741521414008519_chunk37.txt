3: Anesthesia for Surgical Débridement
We recommend that local anesthesia (topical or local injection) be administered to minimize discomfort associated with surgical venous leg ulcer débridement. In selected cases, regional block or general anesthesia may be
required. [GRADE - 1; LEVEL OF EVIDENCE - B]
