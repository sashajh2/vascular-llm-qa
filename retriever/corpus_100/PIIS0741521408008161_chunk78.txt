RECOMMENDATION
We recommend against carotid artery stenting for asymptomatic patients with carotid artery stenosis. (GRADE 1, low
quality evidence).
Evidence
Paucity of evidence hampers the evaluation of carotid
artery stenting in the management of patients with asymptomatic carotid disease. No RCTs have been published comparing
carotid stenting with medical management in asymptomatic
