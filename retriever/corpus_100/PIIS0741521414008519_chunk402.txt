Guideline 4.16: Periulcer Skin Management
We suggest application of skin lubricants underneath compression to reduce dermatitis that commonly
affects periulcer skin. [GRADE - 2; LEVEL OF EVIDENCE - C] In severe cases of dermatitis associated
with venous leg ulcers, we suggest topical steroids to
reduce the development of secondary ulcerations and
to reduce the symptoms
